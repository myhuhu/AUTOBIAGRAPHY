Childhood

I started school at the age of 5 years in a local primary school called Okok primary school. I was a bright student and I was always eager to learn new things at school. I consistently performed well in academics, earning top grades and recognition for my achievements. One notable learning experience was when I participated in a science fair and built a working model of a wind turbine. This project taught me the importance of research, creativity, and perseverance. I also actively participated in extracurricular activities such as sports, drama, and music, which helped me develop teamwork and leadership skills.
